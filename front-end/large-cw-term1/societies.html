Clubs/Societies

Since joining Goldsmiths University I have joined both the anime society and a running group.

Anime

I have liked anime for a couple of years mainly because I appreciate the artistic work that goes into it, the plot and that they have many genres to choose from. Personally my favourite genres are horror/psychological/adventure. I have probably met the most people from this society and we always go on weekly events whether its going to the pub just for a meetup, watching anime screenings or outside events. Some events I have been on include: cinema screenings, ice skating at Winter Wonderland (Hyde Park), visit to China town and MCM London Comic Con.

Running

I enjoy running with my group as it has enabled me to get to know other people outside of my personal circles or in my year. I liked to at least think about my fitness a little especially since I do spend a lot of time around computers and its great to get a change of scenery and to get a breath of fresh air.

Hacksmiths

Hacksmiths is the technology related society I occasionally go to this if there is an interesting topic relating to my field of interest. One of the last events I went to involved github and how to version our work which was very helpful.
